MASALA PAPAD

Description

One of the Most Popular starters in an Indian Restaurant. Very easy to make requiring only a few Ingredients

It is Light, It is Crunchy. Papad is usually made from rice or lentil flour and served Roasted or Fried

INGREDIENTS (1 Serving)

  • 1 Papad
  • 2-3 tbpsn Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Red Chilli Powder
  • Chat Masala
  • Half Chopped Onion
  • Half Chopped Tomato
  • Fresh Coriander
  • Sev

RECIPE

  1. Heat the Oil and Deep Fry the Papad. Remove the Papad From Oil
  2. Add Salt and Pepper According to taste ( usually A pinch of Salt and Pepper is Enough.)
  3. Add Chopped Onions and Tomatoes. Spread it all over the Papad.
  4. Add Sev on top of Onions and Tomatoes.
  5. Top it off with some Chat Masala, Red Chilli Powder and Fresh Coriander
